Aconitum

Other Name:

Ranunculus

Early Spring

Sun:

Partial Shade

Soil:

Well-Drained

Zones:

4 - 9

More:

Aconitum is also referred to as Wolf's bane or Wolfsbane. Their nickname stems from the fact that most types of Aconitum are extremely poisonous. They grow from perennial plants in the Northern Hemisphere and there are over 250 species.

Other Name:

Partial Shade

Well-Drained

10 - 12

More:

If indoors or in a steady climate, the anthurium plant can bloom all year. If you're new to plant care, the anthurium plant may not be for you though. With too little or too much sun, they can begin to turn brown or stop blooming. If you're inexperienced in caring for flower, but really want an anthurium plant, choose a potted variety. Potted anthurium plants can be easily moved so that you can test and choose the best area for it to grow.
