Anthurium

Other Name:

Season:

All Seasons

Sun:

Partial Shade

Soil:

Well-Drained

Zones:

10 - 12

More:

If indoors or in a steady climate, the anthurium plant can bloom all year. If you're new to plant care, the anthurium plant may not be for you though. They're a bit tricky to care for and can be difficult to get to bloom. The lighting you pick for your anthurium plants is very important. With too little or too much sun, they can begin to turn brown or stop blooming. If you're inexperienced in caring for flower, but really want an anthurium plant, choose a potted variety. Potted anthurium plants can be easily moved so that you can test and choose the best area for it to grow.
