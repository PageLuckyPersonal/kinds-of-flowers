Aconitum

Other Name:

Ranunculus

Season:

Early Spring

Sun:

Partial Shade

Soil:

Well-Drained

Zones:

4 - 9

More:

Aconitum is also referred to as Wolf's bane or Wolfsbane. Their nickname stems from the fact that most types of Aconitum are extremely poisonous. They grow from perennial plants in the Northern Hemisphere and there are over 250 species.

African Daisy

Other Name:

Ganzania

Season:

Mid Summer/Fall

Sun:

Full Exposure

Soil:

Well-Drained

Zones:

9 - 11

More:

Native to south Africa, the African daisy flower is a weather resistant flower that can grow in hot and dry conditions. In comes in yellow, a creamy white, purple, orange, and red, but there are some different hybrid forms too.
